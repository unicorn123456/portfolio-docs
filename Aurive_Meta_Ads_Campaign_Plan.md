# Aurive — Meta Ads campaign plan

**Author:** Ikbal Ismail
**Status:** Planned, pending budget approval
**Date:** June 2026

---

This is a campaign brief written before launch, to define targeting, creative, and budget logic ahead of spending. No budget has been committed yet — this plan is what would be executed once a small test budget (suggested: 100 SEK) is approved.

---

## Why Meta Ads, why now

**Organic reach alone isn't enough yet**
Aurive's Instagram is early-stage with limited followers and post history, so organic reach is naturally small. A tiny paid test lets messaging get in front of the right audience without waiting for organic growth to compound.

**Outreach findings already point to the angle**
Direct clinic outreach surfaced two reactions: some clinics found VårdAI "redundant," others said it was "saving us time." This campaign should test messaging that leads with time-saving and after-hours coverage — the angle that already resonated in real conversations.

---

## Campaign specification

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| Objective | Traffic to aurive.se (trackable via the GA4 property already installed) |
| Budget | 100 SEK test, run over 3–5 days to get a meaningful read at small spend |
| Audience | Stockholm, age 28–55, interests: dental clinics, healthcare management, small business owners |
| Placement | Instagram feed + Stories (highest-intent, lowest-cost placements for a cold local audience) |
| Creative | Single image or short video highlighting "24/7 patient booking, even in Arabic" — the differentiator that got positive reactions in outreach |
| CTA | "Learn more" → links to aurive.se, trackable as a GA4 conversion event |
| Success metric | Cost per click under ~5 SEK would be considered a viable signal to scale further |

---

## Why this approach over the alternatives

**Why not boost an existing post**
Boosting is faster but offers weaker targeting and creative control than a proper Ads Manager campaign — not worth the trade-off even at this budget size.

**Why 100 SEK and not more**
At this stage, the goal is directional signal (does the messaging get clicks at all), not statistically reliable conversion data. A larger budget is unjustified until the cheap test validates the angle.
